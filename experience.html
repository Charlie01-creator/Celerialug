Your Journey With Us

Follow the timeline of your unforgettable Celestial Africa hot air balloon safari experience.

Sunrise Preparation

Your adventure begins before dawn. We welcome you with refreshments as you watch the magnificent balloons being inflated against the backdrop of an African sunrise. Our expert team provides a comprehensive safety briefing.

Magical Ascent

As the sun begins to rise, you'll gently lift off the ground. The world becomes quiet except for the occasional burst of the burner. The African landscape unfolds beneath you in a spectacular panorama of colors and textures.

Wildlife Spectacle

From your unique vantage point, witness wildlife in their natural habitat. Elephants, giraffes, and antelope roam below, often unaware of your silent presence above. Your pilot provides expert commentary on the animals and landscape.

Champagne Celebration

After approximately one hour in the air, we land and celebrate your journey with a traditional champagne ceremony. Toast to your adventure as we share stories of the flight and present you with a personalized flight certificate.

Gourmet Bush Breakfast

The experience concludes with a delicious gourmet breakfast prepared in the bush. Enjoy a meal surrounded by nature as you reflect on your incredible journey through the skies. Our chefs use fresh local ingredients to create a memorable dining experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hot air balloon safari experience.

How long is the balloon flight?

The flight itself lasts approximately 1 hour, but the entire experience from preparation to breakfast takes about 3-4 hours. We recommend allowing half a day for the complete Celestial Africa experience.

What should I wear for the flight?

We recommend wearing comfortable, layered clothing as it can be cool in the early morning but warmer as the sun rises. Closed-toe shoes are recommended. Avoid loose items that could be carried away by wind.

Are there any age or health restrictions?

Children should be at least 8 years old and accompanied by an adult. Participants should be able to stand for the duration of the flight and manage a step into the basket. Those with serious health conditions, recent surgeries, or who are pregnant should consult their doctor before booking.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unsuitable for flying, we will reschedule your flight for the next available date or provide a full refund. The final decision to fly is made by our pilot on the morning of your scheduled flight.

Can I bring a camera on the flight?

Absolutely! We encourage guests to bring cameras to capture the breathtaking views. Our baskets are designed with photography in mind, providing stable platforms and unobstructed views. We recommend bringing a strap for your camera for added security.
