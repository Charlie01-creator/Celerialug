Your Journey With Us

Sunrise Preparation

Your adventure begins before dawn. We welcome you with refreshments as you watch the magnificent balloons being inflated. Our expert team provides a comprehensive safety briefing.

Magical Ascent

As the sun begins to rise, you'll gently lift off the ground. The world becomes quiet except for the occasional burst of the burner. The African landscape unfolds beneath you in a spectacular panorama.

Wildlife Spectacle

From your unique vantage point, witness wildlife in their natural habitat. Elephants, giraffes, and antelope roam below, often unaware of your silent presence above.

Champagne Celebration

After approximately one hour in the air, we land and celebrate your journey with a traditional champagne ceremony. Toast to your adventure as we share stories of the flight.

Gourmet Bush Breakfast

The experience concludes with a delicious gourmet breakfast prepared in the bush. Enjoy a meal surrounded by nature as you reflect on your incredible journey through the skies.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the balloon flight?

The flight itself lasts approximately 1 hour, but the entire experience from preparation to breakfast takes about 3-4 hours.

What should I wear for the flight?

We recommend wearing comfortable, layered clothing as it can be cool in the early morning but warmer as the sun rises. Closed-toe shoes are recommended.

Are there any age or health restrictions?

Children should be at least 8 years old and accompanied by an adult. Participants should be able to stand for the duration of the flight and manage a step into the basket. Those with serious health conditions should consult their doctor.
