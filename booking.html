Booking FAQs

Find answers to common questions about booking your hot air balloon safari with Celestial Africa.

How far in advance should I book my hot air balloon safari?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-February and June-September). For holiday periods and special occasions, we suggest booking 2-3 months ahead to secure your preferred date.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 7 days before your scheduled flight for a full refund. Cancellations between 2-7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. In case of weather-related cancellations, we offer a full refund or rescheduling.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), bank transfers, and mobile money payments. A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking, with the balance due 7 days before your flight. For last-minute bookings (within 7 days), full payment is required.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 6 or more. For groups of 6-8, we provide a 10% discount. Groups of 9-12 receive a 15% discount. Private charters are also available for special occasions and corporate events.
