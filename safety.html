Weather Safety Policy

We maintain conservative weather standards to ensure optimal flying conditions and maximum safety margins.

Optimal Conditions

Winds under 8 knots, clear visibility, stable atmospheric conditions. 95% of flights proceed as scheduled.

Marginal Conditions

Winds 8-12 knots, light precipitation possible. Flights may be delayed or rescheduled for safety.

No-Fly Conditions

Winds over 12 knots, precipitation, thunderstorms, or poor visibility. Flights are cancelled and rescheduled.

Weather Decision Process

Our pilots make the final flight decision based on comprehensive weather data and real-time conditions. This decision is made on the morning of each flight, considering:

  • Surface wind speed and direction
  • Wind patterns at flight altitudes
  • Visibility and cloud cover
  • Precipitation and storm activity
  • Atmospheric stability

Safety FAQs

Common questions about our safety procedures and protocols.

How safe is hot air ballooning compared to other aviation?

Hot air ballooning is statistically one of the safest forms of aviation. The simple technology, lack of complex moving parts, and conservative operating practices contribute to an excellent safety record. At Celestial Africa, we maintain a 100% safety record through rigorous procedures and experienced pilots.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We continuously monitor weather conditions using advanced meteorological tools. If conditions are unsuitable, we will reschedule your flight for the next available date or provide a full refund. The final decision to fly is made by our pilot on the morning of your scheduled flight based on real-time conditions.

What medical conditions might prevent someone from flying?

Participants should be able to stand unaided for approximately one hour and manage a step into the basket. Those with serious heart conditions, recent surgeries, mobility limitations, or who are pregnant should consult their doctor before booking. We recommend discussing any concerns with our team when making your reservation.

What safety equipment is on board?

All our balloons carry comprehensive safety equipment including: fire extinguishers, first aid kits, emergency locator transmitters, multiple communication devices, and emergency supplies. Our baskets are specifically designed for stability and safety with reinforced construction and secure handholds.

How often are your balloons inspected?

We conduct thorough inspections before every flight. Additionally, our balloons undergo detailed maintenance checks every 100 hours of operation and comprehensive annual inspections that exceed manufacturer recommendations. All maintenance is documented and tracked in our safety management system.
