Hot air ballooning is weather-dependent. We may cancel flights due to:

In case of weather cancellation, we offer:

  1. Rescheduling to the next available date
  2. Full refund if rescheduling is not possible
  3. Credit voucher for future use

4. Safety Requirements

4.1 Passenger Requirements

For safety reasons, all passengers must:

  • Be at least 7 years old
  • Be able to stand unaided for the duration of the flight (approx. 1 hour)
  • Be able to enter and exit the basket without assistance
  • Not be pregnant
  • Not have serious medical conditions without doctor's clearance
  • Not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs

4.2 Health Declaration

Passengers must disclose any medical conditions, disabilities, or medications that may affect their ability to participate safely. We reserve the right to refuse participation to anyone we deem unfit to fly for safety reasons.

4.3 Safety Briefing

All passengers must attend the pre-flight safety briefing. Failure to do so may result in exclusion from the flight without refund. Passengers must follow all instructions from the pilot and crew at all times.
